Anna Keller

Client Coordinator

Anna is an integral part of the s3 team and acts as a client liaison, specifically in coordinating and monitoring various procedures. In her role, responsibilities include providing on-going support to contractors/vendors involved with current projects, facilitating and coordinating meetings with contractors and tenants, oversight of design meetings to assure scope and content are clearly outlined to minimize change orders, maintaining activity checklists for each project to sustain predictable completion of critical schedule milestones, managing local municipality relationships and permit process, managing and maintaining all audit-required documents.

In a construction career that has spanned approximately 10 years, Anna was an Assistant Project Manager for a Midwest multi-family developer. During this time, she managed to fine-tune her skills by assisting with the estimating process for budget and Scope of Work for the lead Estimator, coordinating with Construction Management team to ensure a streamlined transition and completed understanding of any given Project, leading discussions regarding pre-construction planning, developing and implementing project procedures, and detailed review of the Scope of Work, Contract Timelines, Benchmarks, and Payment Schedules and Permits required.

Qualities such as self-motivation, strong communication, and a bit of humor have enabled Anna to be a fundamental component of the value chain in the overall tenant experience that s3 provides. Quick fact, Anna is fluent in Polish and is a ballet dancer.
